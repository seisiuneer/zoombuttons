  • The Ould Sod Virtual Session on Zoom, PLACEHOLDER, Thursday, PLACEHOLDER, starting at 6:00 PM Pacific time!
  • “The Ould Sod” is the San Diego neighborhood pub where I’ve been co-hosting a traditional Irish session every Tuesday night for over 20 years.
  • Here’s how it works:
  • Anyone on the meeting may volunteer to start a set.
  • I mute everyone’s microphones except the person playing the set.
  • The person plays their set, and everyone else can hear them and play along.
  • Because only one mike is un-muted, you hear only the person who started the set and yourself.
  • When done with the set, I will un-mute all the microphones (for those who have allowed the meeting host to un-mute).
  • At that point anyone can pick a new set to play and I do the same muting setup as before for the next set leader.
  • We also do "chained" sets where multiple players play one tune three times and then it moves on to the next player as well as "Celtic Karaoke™" sets where players mix in recordings along with their live performance.
  • Come join us to play or feel free to just listen all night!
